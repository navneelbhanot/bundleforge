- One voice talent; no music under the VO; ambient riser starts at 0:35.

## Recording

- Browser: Chrome 1280×720, scaled to 1080p in post.
- Cursor highlight on; click sounds off.
- Use the demo store; reset right before recording so demo data is
  pristine.
- Two takes: one with cursor, one without (for B-roll).
